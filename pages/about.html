Our Story

A Legacy Built on Excellence

Scootid Academy was founded in 2005 by a group of passionate educators who shared a bold vision: to build a school in Rivers State that would rival the very best in Nigeria and beyond.

Starting with just 60 pupils in a modest building on Captain Allison Close, Eneka, the academy has grown into a thriving community of over 1,200 students, 80+ qualified staff, and a campus equipped with modern laboratories, libraries, and digital learning centres.

Over the past two decades, Scootid Academy has consistently produced top-performing students in WAEC, NECO, and JAMB examinations, with alumni now working as doctors, engineers, lawyers, and entrepreneurs across Nigeria and the world.

Our Core Values

These values are not merely words on a wall. They are the living principles that guide every interaction, every lesson, and every decision at Scootid Academy.

01 · Value
🏅 Academic Excellence

We hold every student to the highest academic standards — challenging them to achieve their very best through rigorous curriculum, dedicated teaching, and continuous assessment.

02 · Value
🛡️ Integrity & Honour

We cultivate honesty, trustworthiness, and moral courage. Students learn that true character is defined by what they do when no one is watching.

03 · Value
💡 Innovation

We embrace technology, creativity, and new ways of thinking. Our ICT labs, coding clubs, and science olympiads ensure students are prepared for tomorrow's world.

04 · Value
💛 Compassion

We nurture empathy, kindness, and community spirit. Our students are taught to serve their communities and to see themselves as contributors to a better society.

05 · Value
📐 Discipline

Self-discipline is the bedrock of success. Through structured routines, clear expectations, and mentorship, we help students develop the habits of high achievers.

06 · Value
🌍 Global Citizenship

We raise students who are aware of the world beyond their community — grounded in Nigerian identity, yet ready to compete and contribute on the world stage.
