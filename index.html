About the School

A Legacy of Academic Excellence & Character

Scootid Academy was founded with a singular vision: to create a nurturing centre of academic brilliance in the heart of Rivers State. For over two decades, we have consistently produced graduates who excel in universities across Nigeria and beyond.

Our approach blends rigorous academics with deep moral formation, equipping every child not only with knowledge, but with the wisdom, discipline, and leadership skills to make a meaningful impact in the world.
